TUP-TAGUIG

The TUP-Taguig is rooted from history as the Manila Technician Institute (MTI), one of the three prototype technician institutes established by the National Government in 1977. In 1978, PCAT was granted its university status, renamed into the Technological University of the Philippines and was designated as the apex of technology education in the country. This development brought the MTI under the management of the TUP and its eventual renaming as TUP-Taguig in 1985. Today, the TUP-Taguig is the main provider of technology education in the southeastern part of Metro Manila providing engineers, technicians and skilled workers for the industries in the area.
